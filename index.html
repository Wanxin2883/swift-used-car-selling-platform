COMMON FAQS

How do I purchase a membership?

+ -

Select the desired membership, provide your information and payment details, and complete the purchase. Once done, you'll receive a confirmation with your membership details, allowing you to access the website's features and services for selling your car.

Which platforms are supported?

+ -

Our website supports web browsers, mobile devices (iOS and Android), and desktop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux). We also have optimized versions or dedicated apps for tablets.

How do I make payment for the booked car?

+ -

To make a payment for a booked car, contact the seller to inquire about their accepted payment methods. Choose a suitable method such as bank transfer, or online payment platforms, and follow the our website's instructions to complete the payment securely.

How to cancel the subscription?

+ -

To cancel your subscription with Swift, log in to your account and access the account settings or subscription management section. Locate the option to cancel your subscription and follow the provided instructions, which include confirming your decision and completing additional steps. Keep a copy of the cancellation confirmation for your records and consider canceling before the renewal date to avoid any further charges.
