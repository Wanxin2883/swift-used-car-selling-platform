ABOUT US

  • Small Startup Company

    Swift started off as a small startup company created by four friends in order to make renting cars for students at HELP University more convenient. The company started expanding to other universities in Malaysia as the website improved.

  • Leap of Faith

    Recently, Swift has taken a leap of faith to venture into the business globally throughout the whole country. Hence, they needed to render the website to fulfill the needs of every customer throughout the country.

  • Increasing Demand

    Due to increasing demand from customers, Swift now offers used car sales to their customers in addition to automobile rentals.

VISION

Empowering university students with accessible and dependable transportation options, enabling them to focus on their education and create lasting memories.

MISSION

To provide affordable and reliable used cars for university students, ensuring convenient and safe transportation throughout their academic journey.
